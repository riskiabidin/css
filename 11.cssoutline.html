CSS Outline

An outline is a line that is drawnn around elements, OUTSIDE the borers, to make the elements "stand out"

CSS has the following outline properties:

  • outline-style
  • outline-color
  • outline-width
  • outline-offset
  • outline

Outline differs form borders! Unlike border, the outline is drawn outside the element's border, and may overlap other content. Also, the outline is NOT a part of the element's dimensions;the element's total width and height is not affected by width of the outline.
